## What You Can Build

Using UnPay APIs, you can:

1. Accept payments via UPI, cards, and net banking
2. Initiate and manage payouts to bank accounts and beneficiaries
3. Verify user identities with KYC services (PAN, Aadhaar, bank validation)
4. Automate transaction workflows using real-time webhooks
5. Build and scale fintech products such as marketplaces, wallets, lending systems, and neobanking platforms

## Product **Suite**

UnPay provides a modular suite of APIs that cover the full lifecycle of financial operations—from payment collection to fund disbursement, identity verification, and banking infrastructure.

Each product is designed to operate independently while maintaining consistency across the platform.
